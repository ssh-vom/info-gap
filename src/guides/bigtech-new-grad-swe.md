## The resume black hole is real

Most people apply and hear nothing. The fix isn't a better resume — it's
referrals and volume. I applied to 140 companies, got 6 onsites, 2 offers.

## Referral math

One referral ≈ 20 cold applications, statistically. So spend your time on:
1. LinkedIn alumni search filtered by "McMaster University" + target company.
2. Cold DM with a *specific* question, not "can you refer me."
3. Ask for a referral only after a real back-and-forth.

## What I wish I'd done in 2nd year

Start the easy Leetcode grind then, not in 4th year panic mode. One problem a
day. By the time you're applying, you've seen the patterns.
